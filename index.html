How to Use — Vintage Cube Tier Maker

Getting Started

Click + New List, choose Vintage Cube, and give it a name. ~370 cards load automatically from Scryfall with full art and metadata.

Sorting the Pool

Use the sort bar above the Unassigned section to organize cards before ranking:

  • 🎨 Color — groups by W → U → B → R → G → Colorless → Multi → Land
  • CMC ↑ — Lands first, then 0-cost, then ascending mana value
  • A→Z — alphabetical  ·  ↺ Shuffle — random order

Assigning Cards to Tiers

Drag any card from the Unassigned pool into a tier row (S / A / B / C / F). Or click a card to zoom in, then click a tier button or press its letter key.

Quick Rank

Click ▶ Quick Rank to review unassigned cards one at a time. Press a tier letter (S A B C F) to assign, or press → / Skip → to defer the card to the end of the queue.

Sort Within a Tier

Click ↕ on any tier row to open the Sort Wizard. Two cards appear — pick which is better:

  • Left-hand keys (Q A Z W S X E D C R F V T, 1–5) → left card is better
  • Right-hand keys (P L O K M I J N U, 7–0 - = [ ] ' / ; . ,) → right card is better
  • Or click ▲ Pick under either card

Each card is sorted via binary search — up to 4 comparisons per card. Cards already sorted stay in position.

Comparing Lists

Click Compare ↔ to open the compare view (only lists that are fully ranked and locked are eligible). Use the All Cards / Head to Head toggle to switch between two modes:

  • All Cards — every card sorted by average rank across all eligible lists. Each row shows a horizontal bar with a colored dot per list at that card's position (#1 = best, left; #N = worst, right). Hover a dot to see the exact rank number.
  • Head to Head — pick two lists from the dropdowns to compare their top 10 cards as card-image thumbnails side by side.

Click ← Back to return to your tier list. Loading any list also returns you automatically.

Other Controls

  • ⚖ Normalize — redistributes cards proportionally across tiers
  • 🔒 Lock — prevents accidental changes to tier assignments
  • Search bar — highlights matching cards in the pool
  • Escape — closes any open overlay
